Reading now: Derek Jeter lists his Tampa mansion for $29 MILLION after renting it to Tom Brady and Gisele

Derek Jeter, 46, and his wife Hannah have reportedly listed their massive 30,000-square foot mansion near Tampa for an eye-popping $29 million, according to the New York Times on Tuesday.

Jeter's company first purchased three adjacent lots on Davis Islands, an exclusive Florida enclave, back in 2005 and 2006 for $7.7 million and completed construction on the home in 2011. 

Currently, the stunning property is being rented by NFL star Tom Brady, wife Gisele and their children, who moved in after the 43-year-old inked a $50 million two-year deal with the Tampa Bay Buccaneers earlier this year.

'This is the most impressive home to ever hit the market in the Tampa Bay area,' Stephen Gay, the Smith & Associates Real Estate agent who has the listing told the New York Times.

The sprawling waterfront estate spreads out over 1.25 acres of land on Hillsborough Bay and is a combination of three adjacent lots that Jeter's company bought about 15 years ago.

With a price tag nearing $30 million, prospective buyers are in for a one-of-a-kind space which was custom built for the former New York Yankee.

The 30,875-square-foot space boasts seven bedrooms, 16 bathrooms (eight full baths and eight half-baths), gourmet kitchen, wine cellar, gym and movie theater.
