Reading now: Trump staffer is slammed for mocking Joe Biden as he visited graves of his loved ones

A staffer for Donald Trump is under fire for mocking Joe Biden as he visited the graves of his late family members over the weekend. 

On Sunday Francis Brennan, Trump's campaign director of strategic response, slammed Biden for not speaking to media, as he walked through Brandywine church's cemetery in Wilmington, Delaware, where his late son Beau is buried along with his first wife Neilia and their daughter Naomi.

Brennan tweeted video of of Biden walking through the cemetery. A reporter is heard saying: 'Mr. Vice President come talk to us!' and Biden responds with a cheerful wave before walking along to pay his respects to his loved ones.

Brennan sarcastically captioned the video: 'Joe Biden just keeps meandering along.'

Francis Brennan, Trump's campaign director of strategic response, mocked Joe Biden on Twitter on Sunday as he visited the graves of his loved ones. He said that 'Joe Biden just keeps meandering along' when reporters asked to speak with him. Biden politely waved to reporters in the clip

On Sunday Biden attended church then visited Brandywine church's cemetery in Wilmington, Delaware, where his late son Beau is buried along with his first wife Neilia and their daughter Naomi

Beau Biden, the former Delaware attorney general and an Iraq veteran, died from brain cancer in 2015 at the age of 46. Neila and Naomi Biden died in a car accident in 1972.

The incident came days after it was revealed the president allegedly refused to visit a war cemetery in Paris back in 2018 and said Americans who died in wars were 'losers' and 'suckers' – but Trump denies the report.

As Biden attended church and visited his loved ones, Trump spent Saturday and Sunday playing golf at the Trump National Golf Club in Sterling, Virginia.

Brennan’s tweet was met with bitter backlash by Twitter users to said he was disrespecting Biden in his emotional visit to his family's graves.
