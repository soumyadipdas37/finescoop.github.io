Reading now: Today show hosts Karl Stefanovic and Allison Langdon BLAST Ellen DeGeneres apology

Today show hosts Karl Stefanovic and Allison Langdon have slammed Ellen DeGeneres' apology following allegations that her show harboured a toxic workplace.

On Monday, the US comedienne kicked off the 18th season of her daytime talk show by telling her virtual audience she was 'taking responsibility' of the damning claims while also acknowledging she was not a behind-the-scenes villain that several reports had suggested.

However, Allison lashed out the 62-year-old for the 'weird and uncomfortable' comedic undertones in her five-minute monologue while Ellen super-fan Karl suggested it was time for her to 'go away and re-establish her connection with the people who mattered'.
