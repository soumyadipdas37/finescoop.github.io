Reading now: Third times a charm! Yolanda Hadid wishes her true love Joseph Jingoli a happy birthday

Yolanda Hadid seems to have found 'true love' with boyfriend of over a year, Joseph Jingoli. 

The former Real Housewife of Beverly Hills, 56, and mom to famous models Bella, 23, and Gigi Hadid, 25, has previously been married twice but is clearly smitten with her new businessman beau.   

On Saturday the former model took to Instagram to wish her man a happy birthday in a sweet tribute post. 

True love: Yolanda Hadid, 56, wishes her boyfriend of over a year Joseph Jingoli a happy birthday in a sweet Instagram tribute. The former Real Housewife of Beverly Hills has previously been married twice 

 'Happy birthday my true love.. May each day, love, health and happiness come your way. Thank you for being the quiet in my storm.... You are a blessing in my life! #JosephJingoli' the Dutch beauty wrote. 

In the photo the two are seen all geared up for a day of horseback riding. 

The pair are clad in blue jeans and riding boots as they wear matching cowboy hats for a day on the riding trail. 

In the second photo Yolanda posts a snap of her man on horseback as the two share the mutual hobby. 

The post quickly garnered celebratory comments and likes from daughter Gigi and singer Dua Lipa who has been dating Yolanda's son Anwar, 21. 

Horseback love: Yolanda and Joseph lead very compatible farm lifestyles as the two live ten miles from each other in Pennsylvania and spend a lot of their days in the great outdoors

Matching: The pair are clad in blue jeans and riding boots as they wear matching cowboy hats for a day on the riding trail. The two share a mutual love for horses and frequently ride together

 Jingoli and Yolanda were first linked last August after they made it official.  

The investor and CEO has his hands in a number of businesses including an organization called The Farm Team, which helps adults in addiction recovery. 

Yolanda who lives on her farm in Pennsylvania is in close proximity with her boyfriend who reportedly lives just ten miles away. 

The couple were set up by mutual friends and continue to go from strength to strength as they have incredibly compatible lifestyles and he gives her a sense of 'normalcy'. 

Birthday: In January Joseph threw Yolanda a lavish birthday dinner where she was surrounded by her family and friends 

Daughters: Former model Yolanda is mother to Gigi, Bella, and Anwar who are all currently in demand models 

In January, Joseph threw Yolanda a lavish birthday dinner where she was surrounded by her family and friends including model daughters Bella and Gigi. 

Yolanda was previously married to songwriter David Foster for six years before the pair split in 2017. He is now re-married to singer Katharine McPhee. 

Before that she was married to real estate developer Mohamed Hadid with whom she shares her three children. The pair ended things in 2000. 

Goat garden: On her farm Yolanda has virtually every farm animal from horses to goats to ducks and chickens

Yolanda has been living a blissful life on her 32 acre farm since she bought the property in 2017. The mother of three has been very open about her struggles with Lyme Disease and has taken to the east coast for a chiller way of life.

The farm - which is just an hour and a half from NYC where all of her kids reside - has been a home base for the family, especially in quarantine. 

Bella and Gigi spent the majority of the past six months at the farm, documenting their time with the animals and as a family while Gigi has been expecting her first child with longtime love Zayn Malik. 

Anwar spent a lot of time with girlfriend Dua in the UK during quarantine before the two popped back to the US to be together as a family before returning to NYC.
