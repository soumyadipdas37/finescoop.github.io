Reading now: Kim Kardashian, Gabrielle Union send condolences to Chrissy Teigen and John Legend over loss of baby

Chrissy Teigen revealed on Wednesday evening that she and husband John Legend, 41, have lost their baby.

The 34-year-old model announced the news via Instagram and some of the couple's closest pals, like Kim Kardashian and Gabrielle Union, voiced their condolences in the comment section.

'We’re always here for you and love you guys so much,' wrote Kim Kardashian, 39, who happens to share four children of her own with husband Kanye West, 43.

 Tragic: Chrissy Teigen has revealed that she and husband John Legend have lost their baby

Gabrielle Union, 47, made sure that Chrissy and John knew that her family would be there for them during this trying time. 

'We love you guys so much and we will be here for whatever yall need. Always,' she commented.

Paris Hilton, who recently opened up about her own pregnancy plans, said that her 'heart breaks for [Chrissy] and John.'

'I am so sorry for your loss. Sending you and your family so much love. Love you beautiful,' wrote the 39-year-old heiress.

Support: The 34-year-old model announced the news via Instagram and some of the couple's closest pals, like Kim Kardashian and Gabrielle Union, voiced their condolences in the comment section

Though she is not a mother, herself, Hailey Bieber expressed sympathy for John and Chrissy's unfortunate situation in Chrissy's Instagram comment section.

The 23-year-old model, who is married to Justin Bieber, wrote: 'I’m so so sorry. Thinking of you and John, praying for you guys and sending you so much love during this time.'

Channing Tatum let the couple know that he is 'sending so much love to [them] right now,' while Jersey Shore star Snooki informed Chrissy that she is 'praying for [her] and [her] beautiful family.'  

Jesse Tyler Ferguson, who just became a dad for the first time with husband of seven-years Justin Mikita, 35, said that he wish he could 'take away [Chrissy's] heartbreak.'

The 44-year-old Modern Family alum added: 'Sending so much love to you and john and the kids.'

Kim Kardashian's BFF La La Anthony also appeared in Chrissy's comment section with condolences of her own.

'Sending so much love and prayers to you, John, and the family. My heart goes out to you. Praying for peace and healing,' the 39-year-old MTV VJ wrote. 

Selma Blair could help but express her 'deepest sympathies' to Chrissy and John.  

'This grief. This grief. It will hold you and the love will break through. I am so sorry . Your family,' she added.

Saying a prayer: RHOBH star Kyle Richards told Chrissy that she would be 'saying a special prayer for Jack tonight,' in reference to Chrissy' unborn son, whom the couple planned to name Jack

RHOBH star Kyle Richards told Chrissy that she would be 'saying a special prayer for Jack tonight,' in reference to Chrissy' unborn son, whom the couple planned to name Jack. 

Academy Award-winning actress Viola Davis said that she was 'so very sorry Chrissy and John' and that she was sending the couple 'A big virtual hug of love,love,love...and more...'

Olivia Munn said that she was 'sending all [her] love' to the couple, while RHONY star Ramona Singer empathized with Chrissy's experience.

'I'm so sorry. I know how much pain you must be in...' she wrote.

'Babe, I'm so sorry. Sending Love & Light,' commented Vanessa Lachey, 39, along with a red heart emoji.

Alyssa Milano thanked Chrissy sharing her raw emotions and experience with her fans, friends, and family online.

'Thank you for sharing this. I'm sending all the love and strength. I am so sorry,' she commented. 

Olivia Culpo told Chrissy that she was 'sending love, prayers, and positivity' her family's way, while Sophia Bush said that she was 'holding [their] hearts in [her] heart.' 

Sending love: Olivia Culpo told Chrissy that she was 'sending love, prayers, and positivity' her family's way, while Sophia Bush said that she was 'holding [their] hearts in [her] heart'

'I'm so so sorry, this is heartbreaking, sending love,' commented Mad Men star January Jones who is a mother, herself, to nine-year-old son Xander Dane.

In her post shared Wednesday night, Chrissy penned a lengthy caption explaining the tragedy and expressing her family's grief over the sudden loss.

She wrote: 'We are shocked and in the kind of deep pain you only hear about, the kind of pain we’ve never felt before. We were never able to stop the bleeding and give our baby the fluids he needed, despite bags and bags of blood transfusions. It just wasn’t enough.'

Teigen was hospitalized on Sunday night after enduring a month of bleeding due to having a weak placenta and has remained at the hospital since.

 'We are shocked': The 34-year-old model also penned a lengthy caption explaining the tragedy and expressed her family's grief over the sudden loss

She continued: 'We never decide on our babies’ names until the last possible moment after they’re born, just before we leave the hospital. But we, for some reason, had started to call this little guy in my belly Jack.

'So he will always be Jack to us. Jack worked so hard to be a part of our little family, and he will be, forever.' 

Chrissy and John revealed in the music video for John's new single Wild that they were expecting their third child together, a son. 

In undoubtedly the most heartbreaking portion of Teigen's caption, the model penned a message to her unborn son Jack.

In her words: Teigen also penned a lengthy caption explaining the tragedy and expressing her family's grief 

'To our Jack - I’m so sorry that the first few moments of your life were met with so many complications, that we couldn’t give you the home you needed to survive. We will always love you,' she wrote.

'Thank you to everyone who has been sending us positive energy, thoughts and prayers. We feel all of your love and truly appreciate you.

'We are so grateful for the life we have, for our wonderful babies Luna and Miles, for all the amazing things we’ve been able to experience. 

'But everyday can’t be full of sunshine. On this darkest of days, we will grieve, we will cry our eyes out. But we will hug and love each other harder and get through it,' concluded Chrissy.

Forever Jack: 'We never decide on our babies’ names until the last possible moment after they’re born, just before we leave the hospital. But we, for some reason, had started to call this little guy in my belly Jack,' explained Teigen

Teigen and Legend, who wed in 2013, are parents to daughter Luna, four, and son Miles, two.

Shortly after uploading her undeniably sad news to Instagram, Chrissy took to her Twitter page to further express her shock over the loss of their child.

'Driving home from the hospital with no baby. How can this be real,' she wrote.

The four photos uploaded to Chrissy's Instagram paint a heartbreaking scene, with one of the pictures showing Chrissy crying and cradling Jack in her arms as John leaned next to them.

Another featured Chrissy sobbing as she held her hands to her face and sat at the edge of her hospital bed in nothing but a blanket and a bonnet.
