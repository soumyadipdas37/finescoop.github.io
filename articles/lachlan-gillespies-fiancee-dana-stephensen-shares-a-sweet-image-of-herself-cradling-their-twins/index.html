Reading now: Lachlan Gillespies fiancée Dana Stephensen shares a sweet image of herself cradling their twins

Purple Wiggle Lachlan Gillespie and his ballet dancer fiancée Dana Stephensen surprised fans when they welcomed twins last week. 

And on Friday, the mother-of-three reflected on bringing newborn daughters Lulu and Lottie into the world. 

Dana, 34, posted a sweet picture of herself to Instagram cradling their babies on her chest after giving birth, saying it was 'one of the most amazing experiences.' 

 'It was one of the most amazing experiences': On Friday, Lachlan Gillespie's fiancée Dana Stephensen shared a sweet image of herself cradling their twins after giving birth

'One of the most amazing experiences bringing our girls into the world. Doubly blessed,' she captioned the post. 

Dana also posted a photo of her son Jasper, four, who she shares with her photographer ex-husband, Michael Kai.

'And to the most beautiful little boy who made me a mummy - I adore you,' she captioned the post. 

He has two little sisters now! Dana also posted a photo of her son Jasper, four, who she shares with her photographer ex-husband, Michael Kai

Shock arrival! The couple surprised fans when they welcomed twins last week

Last week, Dana and Lachlan surprised fans when they announced they had welcomed twins, having kept the pregnancy under wraps. 

In an Instagram post, Lachlan, 34, shared a photo of his newborn daughters resting with their mother Dana, 34, as he looked on lovingly. 

He wrote in the caption: 'Oh Lulu and Lottie, your mummy and daddy love you with all we have - you are both so beautiful - and I would love to sing to you both forever'. 

Neither Lachlan nor Dana gave any hint that they were expecting, and their social media accounts do not show many recent photos.   

Still friends: Lachlan's ex-wife Emma Watkins (pictured), who is the Yellow Wiggle, and a number of other celebrities wished the couple congratulations

Sweet: The Yellow Wiggle kept it simple posting two purple love heart emoji's in the comments

Lachlan's ex-wife Emma Watkins, who is the Yellow Wiggle, and a number of other celebrities wished the couple congratulations. 

Lachlan and Dana announced the happy news on Instagram, prompting Emma to post two purple love heart emoji's in the comments. 

Meanwhile, musical theatre star Lucy Durak, 37, wrote: 'Oh Lachy! The hugest congratulations to you and Dana and your beautiful family.' 

Red Wiggle Simon Pryce, 48, commented: 'So beautiful. Congratulations.'  

In addition, television presenter Melissa Doyle, 50, also sent her well wishes writing: 'Bless! Such wonderful news. Much love to all of you.' 

Comedian Em Rusciano, 41, also congratulated the couple saying: 'Oh how wonderful.' 

Celeb comments: Following the joyous news, the couple have been inundated with well-wishes from a number of Australian celebrities, including Lucy Durak, Melissa Doyle and Simon Pryce

The couple announced their engagement in April in a sweet Instagram post, with Lachy writing: 'On Thursday night, Dana asked me to marry her at home under a fairy light sky and a very excited little boy in a beautiful bow tie.' 

In the photo, Lachlan was pictured cuddling up to Dana and Dana's son Jasper, four, inside a bedroom festooned with fairy lights. 

The couple, who went public with their relationship in October, beamed with joy as they cuddled up for the milestone photo. 

Lachlan separated from his Wiggles bandmate Emma Watkins (a.k.a. The Yellow Wiggle) in August 2018, after two years of marriage.
