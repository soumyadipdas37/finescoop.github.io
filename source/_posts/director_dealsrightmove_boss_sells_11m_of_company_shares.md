Rightmove boss Peter Brooks-Johnson sold £1.1million of shares in the FTSE 100-listed property site group.

Brooks-Johnson exercised the rights to more than 171,000 shares this week and sold them immediately after.

The 47-year-old, who has been chief executive since 2017, sold the stock at an average price of 615p – netting around £1.1million before tax.
