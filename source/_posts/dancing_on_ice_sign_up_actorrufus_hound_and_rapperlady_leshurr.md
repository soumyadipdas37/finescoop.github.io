Dancing On Ice has reportedly signed actor Rufus Hound and rapper Lady Leshurr to the new 2021 series.

Show bosses are said to be 'delighted' over scooping the comedian, 41, and YouTube star, 30, as they become the latest celebrities to 'join the line-up'. 

Rufus is best known for TV shows such as Hounded and Trollied, he has also appeared on Nevermind the Buzzcocks and Celebrity Juice.

Exciting: Dancing On Ice has reportedly signed actor Rufus Hound (pictured in 2018) and rapper Lady Leshurr to the new 2021 series 

While Lady Leshurr, real name Melesha Katrina O'Garro, is a YouTube rapping sensation with 1.07 million subscribers.

The musician is best known for her Queen's Speech series where she films rap battles in one continuous take around the world.

Lady Leshurr has also supported Nicki Minaj on her world tour, where she took to the stage in Birmingham and Manchester in 2019.

A source told The Sun: 'Dancing On Ice are putting together one of their most eclectic line-ups ever and bosses are delighted to have got Rufus and Lady Leshurr onboard.
