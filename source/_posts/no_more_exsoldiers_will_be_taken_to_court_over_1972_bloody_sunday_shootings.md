No more ex-soldiers will be taken to court over the 1972 Bloody Sunday shootings, Northern Ireland prosecutors announced today.

The Northern Ireland’s Public Prosecution Service said no further former soldiers will be prosecuted over their actions in Londonderry on January 30, when 13 civilians were fatally shot.
