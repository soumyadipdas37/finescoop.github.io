Watch Me (Whip/Nae Nae) rapper Silento is accused of attacking two people in a Los Angeles area home with a hatchet last week, according to officials.

The Atlanta native, whose real name is Richard Lamar Hawk, aimed his hatchet at the people in the dwelling, one of whom was able to remove the weapon from his grasp, prosecutors said.
