The Premier League have confirmed four new positive coronavirus results have been returned from the 2,131 players and club staff tested between September 7-13.

Those who have tested positive will now have to self-isolate for a period of ten days.

It was revealed that just three positive results were recorded in the first round of top-flight testing last week.    

The Premier League have confirmed four new positive coronavirus results from the new round

The identities of those who have contracted the disease will not be made public by the league due to medical confidentiality reasons.

Manchester City confirmed that Riyad Mahrez and Aymeric Laporte were two of the individuals to have tested positive from last week's batch of testing. 

Both players have been forced to undergo a period of quarantine, and the club revealed that neither were showing any symptoms of the virus.
