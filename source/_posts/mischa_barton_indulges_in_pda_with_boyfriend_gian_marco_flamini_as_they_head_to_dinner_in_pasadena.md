On Tuesday, the 34-year-old was spotted out and about in Pasadena, California, with boyfriend Gian Marco Flamini, an actor.  

Famous face: Mischa Barton was spotted heading to dinner in Pasadena, California, on Tuesday wearing a tailored check jacket with black leather trousers and black boots
