Former Blue Heelers star Ditch Davey is set to join the cast of Home and Away.

The 45-year-old actor, who will play neurosurgeon Dr Christian Green, was last week spotted filming scenes with co-star Penny McNamee, who plays Dr Tori Morgan.

The pair, who play love interests on the show, appeared to be share a tense conversation while filming a scene in Sydney's Palm Beach.

Ditch, who is best known for playing Evan Jones on Seven cop drama Blue Heelers, is joining the cast of Home and Away as neurosurgeon Dr Christian Green.

His character is set to arrive at Summer Bay's Northern Districts Hospital at the request of Dr Morgan, who wants him to help with her brother Justin Morgan's spinal tumour diagnosis.

A trailer for the soap hints at a romance between the two doctors, as they are shown passionately kissing in an elevator. 

Steamy: A trailer for the long running drama teases that a romance between Dr Christian and Dr Tori is set to blossom showing the two passionately locking lips in an elevator

Ditch told Digital Spy in June he was thrilled to be part of the long-running drama.

'Home and Away has been such an institution to audiences in both Australia and the UK for such a long time and it's great to be a part of that now, but the people are what it's all about,' he said.

'The cast and crew are brilliant and have made me feel at home immediately.'
