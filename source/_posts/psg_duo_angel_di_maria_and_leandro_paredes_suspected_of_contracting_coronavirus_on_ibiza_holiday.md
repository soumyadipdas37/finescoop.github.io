Paris Saint-Germain stars Angel Di Maria and Leandro Paredes have cut short their holidays in Ibiza to return to the club and undergo coronavirus testing, according to sources in France. 

L'Equipe claim the pair, who were permitted to visit the Balearic island after PSG's Champions League final defeat by Bayern Munich, have travelled back to the French capital to spend an extended period in isolation.

The report names Mauro Icardi, Ander Herrera, Keylor Navas and Neymar among the other squad members suspected of having contracted Covid-19 in Ibiza.
