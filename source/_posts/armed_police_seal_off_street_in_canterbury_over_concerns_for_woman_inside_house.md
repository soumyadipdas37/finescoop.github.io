Armed police have been called to Canterbury this afternoon, September 30 2020 following a report of concerns for a woman in a property.

The officers are in Monastery Street, with a police helicopter hovering overhead.

Ivy Lane and Longport have been sealed off and officers have weapons trained towards a building which backs onto Monastery Street, according to witnesses at the scene.

Students at the nearby Canterbury College were reportedly advised to stay on the campus as a precautionary measure while the nature of the incident was established.

Witnesses said at least 12 officers were on scene and that a man was seen being led from the house before being put in handcuffs, according to Kent Live.
