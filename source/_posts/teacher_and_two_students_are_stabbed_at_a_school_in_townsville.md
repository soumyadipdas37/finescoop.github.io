A teacher and two students have reportedly been stabbed at a school in Townsville.

The extent of their injuries was not immediately clear. 

Police and paramedics were called to Townsville Community Early Learning Centre at 12.40pm, the Townsville Bulletin reported.  

A person has been arrested, Queensland Police said.
