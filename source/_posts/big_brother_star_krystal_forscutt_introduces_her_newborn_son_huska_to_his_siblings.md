Former Big Brother star Krystal Forscutt welcomed her third child, a son named Huska, on Friday. 

And on Monday, the 34-year-old introduced the newborn to his siblings, Nakoda, two, and Sunny, five. 

In an Instagram Stories video, and multiple photos, the delighted children cuddled up to their new brother, gently cradling him.
