One of the two officers shot during Wednesday night's riots in Louisville has returned to his police  precinct just hours after being released from hospital. 

Major Aubrey Gregory was greeted with a round of applause as he showed up to the Louisville Metro Police's roll call on Thursday morning, less than 24 hours after being shot in the hip. 

Gregory is the commander of Louisville police department's special operations division and was leading the department's response to riots that erupted in Louisville on Wednesday night following a grand jury's decision not to indict any officers on murder charges over the shooting death of black woman, Breonna Taylor.
