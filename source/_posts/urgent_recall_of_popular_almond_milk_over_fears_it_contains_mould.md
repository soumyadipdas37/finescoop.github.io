A popular South Australian almond milk has been recalled from supermarkets due to potential health risks.

Milk Lab Almond Milk 1L is being recalled by Rio Coffee Pty Ltd due to potential microbial contamination.

The milk has a best before date of August 6 2021 and has been available for purchase at cafes, supermarkets and restaurants in South Australia.

Milk Lab Almond Milk 1L with best before date of August 6 2021 has been recalled due to potential contamination

'Food products with microbial contamination may cause illness if consumed,' the recall notice reads.

Microbial contamination is the non-intended addition of microbes including mould, fungi, bacteria and yeast. 

Consumers should return the product to their place of purchase to receive a full refund.

Those concerned about their health should seek medical advice.
