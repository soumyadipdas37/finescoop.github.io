A 14-month old toddler has died after being run over by a car in rural Victoria.

A car was turning toward a slip lane on High Street in Kangaroo Flat, a south-western suburb of Bendigo, when it collided with the child about 7.20pm on Wednesday.

The 14-month-old child suffered critical injuries and was rushed to hospital and later died. 

A car was turning toward a slip lane on High Street in Kangaroo Flats, 5km south-west of Bendigo's CBD when it collided with the child about 7.20pm on Wednesday 

The 56-year-old driver of the car stopped at the scene to help and was helping police with their investigations. 

A total of 152 people have died on Victoria's roads compared to 188 this time last year. 

Anyone who may have witnessed the tragedy or have dash cam footage is urged to come forward or contact Crime Stoppers on 1800 333 000.
