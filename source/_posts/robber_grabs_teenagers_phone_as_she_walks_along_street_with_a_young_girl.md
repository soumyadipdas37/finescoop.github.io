A robber has been captured stealing a woman's phone as she walked along a street with a young girl in Leeds.

CCTV footage showed a man sneak up behind the woman, 15, before grabbing her in Harehills on September 5 around 2.45pm, according to reports.  

He managed to snatch her mobile phone before running away.

The victim dropped a bag of shopping as she ran after the man, leaving the young girl waiting at the corner of the street. 

It was reported online that members of the public later managed to identify and trace the suspect.

His landlord was allegedly informed of the robbery and the thief was subsequently evicted from his home along with his family, reports claimed.

This has not been confirmed.
