West Ham have confirmed that manager David Moyes, along with two members of the first-team, have tested positive for coronavirus. 

Issa Diop and Josh Cullen, who were originally named in the starting XI for the Carabao Cup clash against Hull on Tuesday night have also contracted COVID-19. All three left the stadium immediately.   

The club twitter account posted saying Issa Diop and Josh Cullen had been replaced in the starting XI by Harrison Ashby and Jack Wilshere, but with no explanation given. David Martin was also a late inclusion on the substitutes bench.
