She's got a heavy schedule on her plate as she prepares to direct two movies back to back.

But Olivia Wilde is making sure to treat herself to some R&R as she juggles work with riasing two young children with partner Jason Sudeikis.

On Monday, the former House star, 36, was spotted once again enjoying a horseback riding session in the Los Angeles suburb of Thousand Oaks.

The great outdoors: Olivia Wilde enjoyed another horseback riding session in the LA suburb of Thousand Oaks on Monday as she took time for some R&R

Wilde was dressed in a black tank top and black jodhpurs and boots for the session.

She wore black gloves and tucked her hair under a black helmet.

She has been spotted riding regularly as she and her family continue to reside in Southern California during the ongoing COVOD-19 pandemic.

They moved west when the coronavirus shut down New York City where they have a home.

Stylish equestrian: The actress and director, 36, opted for an all-black ensemble of tank top, jodhpurs, boots and gloves along with a safety helmet  

In demand: The actress, who found fame on TV's House, garnered critical acclaim for her 2019 feature directorial debut Booksmart and has just been tapped to make a Marvel movie for Sony

In 2019, the actress made her feature directorial debut with the high school comedy Booksmart which garnered plenty of buzz and critical acclaim.

Next up, she's set to helm as well as star in the psychological thriller Don't Worry, Darling about an unhappy 1950s housewife.

And earlier this month, it was announced that Wilde has signed a deal to direct a Marvel movie for Sony Pictures which is believed to center on Spider-Woman.

The in demand star is also attached to direct an as-yet untitled holiday movie for Universal Pictures.
