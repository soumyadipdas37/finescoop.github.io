Scotland began their Nation's League campaign with a 1-1 draw against Israel on Friday night, ending a 10-month wait for international football. 

The hosts opened the scoring at Hampden Park with with Ryan Christie scoring a penalty before Eran Zahavi smacked in a powerful strike after 73 minutes to level. 

The result leaves both sides trailing the Czech Republic after the group 2 leaders' 3-1 victory over Slovakia.
