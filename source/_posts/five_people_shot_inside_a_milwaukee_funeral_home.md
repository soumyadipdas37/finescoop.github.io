Five people were shot inside a Wisconsin funeral home Wednesday, officials say.

The shooting broke out at about 12.45pm at the Serenity Funeral Home located at the 4200 block of West Fond Du Lac Avenue in Milwaukee.

Three men and two women were shot and the state of their injuries are not yet known, according to WISN.

Police are at the scene and sources say there were more than 100 witnesses to the shooting. 

Acting Police Chief Michael Brunson was expected to speak about the shooting at 3.15pm local time.
