Wolves have completed the signing of Barcelona right-back Nelson Semedo for a fee that could rise to £37million. 

The Spanish club announced on Twitter on Wednesday morning that the defender has left the Nou Camp for the Premier League.

He brings the Portuguese cohort of Nuno Espirito Santo's squad back up to 10 following the departure of Diogo Jota.
