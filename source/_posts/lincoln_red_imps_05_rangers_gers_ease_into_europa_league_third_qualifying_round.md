Rangers eased past Gibraltar side Lincoln Red Imps to book a Europa League third qualifying round tie at Dutch side Willem II.

James Tavernier opened the scoring with a stunning free-kick in the 21st minute and Connor Goldson powered in a header just before half-time.

Sub Alfredo Morelos made it three by firing home from close range after Greg Stewart headed Borna Barisic's free kick back across goal. 

Jermain Defoe scored the fourth before Morelos added the fifth.
