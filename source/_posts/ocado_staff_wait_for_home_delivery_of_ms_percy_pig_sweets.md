Ocado staff will have to wait a bit longer for Marks & Spencer's famous Percy Pig sweets to be delivered to their front door. 

The online grocer has temporarily suspended deliveries to its staff as it works its way through a backlog of orders. 

Waiting: The firm has said it has been tackling 'a surge in demand' on the first day of its £1.5billion delivery deal with M&S on Tuesday

The firm has said it has been tackling 'a surge in demand' on the first day of its £1.5billion delivery deal with M&S on Tuesday. 

Ocado had to cancel 'a very small number of orders' as a result of the demand. But it stressed the 'vast majority' of orders have been unaffected.
