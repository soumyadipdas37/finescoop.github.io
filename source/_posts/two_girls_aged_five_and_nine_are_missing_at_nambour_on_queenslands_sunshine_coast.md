A frantic search is underway to find two young girls who went missing earlier this week.

The girls, aged five and nine, were last seen after being dropped at a man's house at Nambour, on the Sunshine Coast.

They were last seen around 9.30am on Tuesday. 

The girls, aged five and nine, (pictured) were last seen after being dropped at a man's (pictured) house at Nambour, on the Sunshine Coast

A 41-year-old man, who is known to the girls, has also been reported missing. 

The man is believed to be driving a white 2019 Great Wall dual-cab utility, with Queensland registration 00CDH.   

Anyone who has seen the man or girls or the car, or has knowledge of their whereabouts is being urged to contact police immediately.
