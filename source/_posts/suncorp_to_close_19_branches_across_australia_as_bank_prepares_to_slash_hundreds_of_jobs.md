Suncorp will shut down 19 branches around Australia as part of a major overhaul of the bank.

The closures include nine Queensland branches in response in the coronavirus pandemic. 

The bank announced on Wednesday that over-the-counter transactions had declined by almost one-quarter between June 2019 to June 2020.
