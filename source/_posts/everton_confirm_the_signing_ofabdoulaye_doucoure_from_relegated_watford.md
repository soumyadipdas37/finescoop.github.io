Everton have confirmed the signing of Abdoulaye Doucouré from relegated Watford. 

The French midfielder has joined the Toffees in a deal believed to be worth around £22million after four season with the Hornets. He has signed a three-year deal, with an option for a fourth.

Doucoure's arrival at Goodison Park has topped off a busy couple days at the club, after they confirmed the signing of James Rodriguez from Real Madrid on Monday.
