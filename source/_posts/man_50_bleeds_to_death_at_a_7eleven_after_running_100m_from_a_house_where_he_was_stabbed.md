A man has tragically bled to death at a 7-Eleven petrol station after fleeing a nearby house with multiple stab wounds.   

The 50-year-old was allegedly attacked during a fight with a woman at a Bray Park home in Brisbane's north on Saturday.   

Police were called to reports of an altercation at a Gloucester Crescent residence about 11.30pm. 

A short time later, officer found the man collapsed about 100 metres away at a service station, Nine News reports. 

A 50-year-old man has died from stab wounds at a 7-Eleven (pictured) in Brisbane's north

He was unable to be saved and died at the scene.  

A 41-year-old woman known to the man is assisting detectives.

A crime scene has been declared and investigations are ongoing.
