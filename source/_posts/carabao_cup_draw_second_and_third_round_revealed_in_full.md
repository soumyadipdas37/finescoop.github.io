Liverpool and Manchester United will face lower league opposition in the third round of the Carabao Cup.

Liverpool will face the winners of Bradford City v Lincoln while Manchester United will face the winner of Reading or Luton.   

The second round draw saw League Two side Salford drawn away at Everton and West Brom will host Harrogate.
