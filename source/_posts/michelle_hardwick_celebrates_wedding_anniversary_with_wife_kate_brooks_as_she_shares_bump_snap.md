Michelle Hardwick has marked her one year anniversary with her wife Kate Brooks by sharing a snap of her growing baby bump.

The Emmerdale star, 44, tied the knot with her TV producer other half in a lavish wedding reception at Graceland in Memphis.

The actress, who is due to give birth in October, took to social media and shared a snap of herself showing off her bump with Kate smiling alongside her.

'Here’s to the next chapter Brooksy. We love you so much ❤️ #countingdowntheweeks'.
