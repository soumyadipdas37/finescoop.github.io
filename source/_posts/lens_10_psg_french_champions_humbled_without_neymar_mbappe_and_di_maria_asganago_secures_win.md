Depleted Paris Saint-Germain suffered a shock defeat in their opening Ligue 1 game of the season on Thursday night.

Neymar, Kylian Mbappe, Keylor Navas, Mauro Icardi, Angel Di Maria, Leandro Paredes and Marquinho were all ruled out after positive Covid-19 tests.

And Ignatius Ganago took advantage of a howler by rookie goalkeeper Marcin Bulka to seal shock win for the newly-promoted side.
