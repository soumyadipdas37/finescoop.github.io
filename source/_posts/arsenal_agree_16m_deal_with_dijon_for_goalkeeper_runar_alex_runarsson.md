Arsenal are closing in on a new replacement keeper after agreeing a £1.6million fee with French club Dijon for Runar Alex Runarsson. 

Mikel Arteta is missing a shot-stopper in his squad after the club allowed Emiliano Martinez to mvoe to Premier League rivals Aston Villa this week. 

Runarsson, who had made 36 Ligue 1 appearances for his club, is due in London for a medical today, ahead of completing the switch. 

Runar Alex Runarsson is due in London on Thursday for a medical ahead of joining Arsenal
