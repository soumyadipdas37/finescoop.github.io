Arsenal have completed the signing of Icelandic goalkeeper Alex Runarsson from French side Dijon. 

The Gunners were hunting a back-up goalkeeper following the sale of Emiliano Martinez to Aston Villa last week. 

But Mikel Arteta has got his man, with the 25-year-old signing a four-year deal at the Emirates and is set to play the role of back-up keeper to No 1 Bernd Leno.
