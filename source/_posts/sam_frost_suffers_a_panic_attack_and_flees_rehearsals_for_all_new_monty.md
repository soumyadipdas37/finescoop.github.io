Sam Frost has been open about her struggles with depression and anxiety. 

And during Sunday's episode of The All New Monty: Guys and Gals, the Home And Away star suffered a panic attack during rehearsals for the show. 

'The word sexy has been used a lot and it makes me feel incredibly anxious,' Sam admitted before the dance practice started.

'The fun thing about having anxiety is you never know when it's going to come on. I think performing in front of the group made me feel incredibly anxious.
