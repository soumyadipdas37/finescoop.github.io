A serious rush-hour crash on the M25 is causing eight-mile tailbacks at the Dartford crossing this morning.

The M25 is closed in both directions following the accident between junction five for the M26 and junction four for Orpington - adding hours to rush hour journeys this morning.

The road has been closed anti-clockwise since 5.25am while emergency services work at the scene.

There is currently eight miles of queues stretching beyond junction three for the Swanley Interchange and up to junction 1a at Dartford. 

Motorists are being advised to plan ahead and follow diversion routes.
