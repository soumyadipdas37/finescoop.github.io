Novak Djokovic put past controversy aside to breeze past Sweden's Mikael Ymer in the first round of the French Open.

In his first Grand Slam match since being disqualified from the US Open after hitting a ball at a line judge, Djokovic won in straight sets. 

His victory came in just an hour and 38 minutes, with the Serbian looking for yet another Grand Slam title.
