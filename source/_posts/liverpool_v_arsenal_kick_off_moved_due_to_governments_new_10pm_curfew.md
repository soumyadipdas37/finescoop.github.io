Liverpool’s Premier League clash with Arsenal on Monday night is being moved from an 8.15pm kick off to 8pm due to the Government’s lockdown measures on pubs.

The closure of bars at 10pm meant supporters would have potentially missed the final minutes of the match.

But the Premier League are expected to announce that the tie, which will be shown live on Sky Sports, will kick off earlier to avoid this situation.
