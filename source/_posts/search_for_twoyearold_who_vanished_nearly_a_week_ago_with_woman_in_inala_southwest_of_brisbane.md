A desperate search is underway for a two-year-old and 37-year-old woman who disappeared almost a week ago.

The pair were last seen at a Kulgun Circuit address in Inala, south-west of Brisbane, on September 25.

They have not been in contact with family since and their disappearance has been described as out of character. 

The child is described as less than one-metre tall, Caucasian, of slim build with blonde hair and blue eyes.

Queensland Police and family are concerned about the woman and baby girl's welfare and are seeking any information on their whereabouts.
