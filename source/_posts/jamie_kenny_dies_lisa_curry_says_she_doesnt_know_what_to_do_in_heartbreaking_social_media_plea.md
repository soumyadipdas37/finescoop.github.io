Devastated Lisa Curry has shared a heartbreaking plea to her friends and family, just days after burying her eldest daughter Jaimi Kenny. 

Posting to Instagram on Tuesday, the Olympic swimmer shared a throwback photo of herself tenderly hugging Jamie - who died at the age of 33 last Monday after battling alcoholism and an eating disorder for years.  

In a gut-wrenching caption, Lisa wrote: 'I don’t know what to do.....', alongside emojis depicting a sad face, an a broken heart and an angel.
