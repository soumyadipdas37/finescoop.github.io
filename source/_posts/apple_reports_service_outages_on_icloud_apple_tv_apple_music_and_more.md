Apple engineers were working on Tuesday night to repair widespread outages across their systems, affecting iCloud, the App Store and Apple TV, among other services.

It was not clear what was causing the disruption, first reported around 8pm EST on Tuesday. 

Apple's dashboard, showing the status of their services, was alight with red warning signs, indicating outages.

Apple's dashboard on Tuesday was showing outages across the system 

Apple TV, Apple Arcade, Apple Books, Apple TV, iCloud and iTunes were just some of the services affected.

Some by Tuesday evening had already been repaired - two Apple Cash apps - and others were unaffected, such as Siri, Apple Maps and iMessage.
