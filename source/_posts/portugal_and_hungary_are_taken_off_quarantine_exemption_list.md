Portugal and Hungary have today been removed from the quarantine exemption list, meaning holidaymakers must rush home or face a two-week quarantine. 

Those arriving in England from the popular locations alongside French Polynesia and Reunion must self-isolate for 14-days from 4am on Saturday, Transport Secretary Grant Shapps said.
