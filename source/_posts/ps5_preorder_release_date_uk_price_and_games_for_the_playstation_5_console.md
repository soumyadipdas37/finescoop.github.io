Playstation 5 (PS5) pre-orders launched on Amazon today at 9 am and sold out within 40 minutes. The £449 Sony Console and £359.99 Digital Edition console will be released on November 12, 2020, but you can still receive an email to notify you about when the item becomes available again.

You can also register your interest on Very, ShopTo and Argos is set to open up its pre-orders this Friday (September 18). 

If PS5 pre-orders become available again on Amazon, you'll be eligible for Pre-order Price Guarantee. This means you'll pay the lowest price offered by Amazon UK between the time you place your order up to and including the end of the day of the release date.

PS5 launch title games will include Demon's Souls, Destruction AllStars, Marvel's Spider-Man: Miles Morales and Sackboy A Big Adventure.

Need an extra controller? Sony has announced the DualSense controller will cost £59.99, while the DualSense charging station, which charges two DualSense wireless controllers, costs £24.99.

The Pulse 3D wireless headset costs £89.99, the PS5 HD camera costs £49.99, and its media remote costs £24.99.
