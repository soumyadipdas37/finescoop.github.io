He has kept to his work out routine ever since his professional football days.

And David Beckham sent his fans wild on Friday as he shared a snap from his early morning work out, while cooling down with a stretch in his home gym.

The former football ace, 45, showcased his muscular physique in a grey vest top and a pair of sports shorts and pale green trainers. 

Wow! David Beckham sent his fans wild on Friday as he shared a snap from his early morning work out, while cooling down with a stretch in his home gym

David finished his look with an orange beanie hat, and admitted in the caption of the snap that it was quite trying for him to get into the position. 

He said: 'Took me a while to get into this stretch but it’s a good one after an early workout before school run. great way to start the day.'

His snap caused a frenzy online as he received a warm reception from his followers who swooned over the athletic snap.
