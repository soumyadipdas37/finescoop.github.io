Ksenija Lukich and her husband Dan Bragg are expecting their first child together in March next year.

And on Wednesday, the former E! Australia presenter flaunted her growing baby bump in a black and white selfie on Instagram. 

The expectant mother, 30, was simply glowing as she posed in her workout clothes.  

Blooming lovely! On Wednesday, former E! Australia host Ksenija Lukich (pictured) proudly showed off her blossoming baby bump on Instagram

'Showing a little earlier than I thought I would. @danbrgg calls it my little pot belly,' she captioned the picture. 

In the image, Ksenija was posing side-on in pair of exercise leggings and a crop top, as she cradled her bump. 

She wore her brunette locks loose and flaunted her stunning looks with minimal makeup. 

Baby on board: Ksenija and her husband Dan Bragg (both pictured) are expecting their first child together in March next year

Ksenija announced her pregnancy on Instagram on Saturday alongside two sweet photos featuring herself and Dan. 

The brunette beauty explained that it took them 'two years' and 'dozens of doctors appointments' but their baby was 'worth the wait'. 

 'Due March 2021,' she captioned the photos.

'After almost two years, dozens of doctors appointments, painful procedures and a lot of tears our little buddy is finally on the way. We can’t wait to meet you #worththewait.' 

In the photos, the couple cuddled up on the beach as they cradled Ksenija's blossoming baby bump. 

A second photo showed Ksenija excitedly jumping up to embrace her lawyer husband. 

Expecting: The soon-to-be mum was glowing in a short floral dress that accommodated her growing bump
