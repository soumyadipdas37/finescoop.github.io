Former Bachelor star Tara Pavlovic has finally married her partner Nick Sherpherdson.

The pair tied the knot in an intimate ceremony on Friday, after being forced to postpone their wedding in April due to the coronavirus pandemic. 

The 30-year-old gushed over Nick as she announced the news on Saturday, before revealing that they fed guests McDonald's at the ceremony.

'I finally got to marry my best friend yesterday, I love you Nick,' Tara captioned a picture of their wedding rings on Saturday. 

In the background was a picture of a lavish food menu from their reception, which included vegetarian pasta, greens and chicken, and a cookie emblazoned with their wedding date and 'Mr and Mrs.'

She also revealed that at the ceremony, guests were fed McDonald's cheeseburgers and an array of other treats from the fast food giant.
