A man is in hospital fighting for his life after the hotel room he was in exploded overnight.

The 52-year-old was the sole occupant in the room at Casa on Gregory, Parap, in Darwin's inner north, when the blast occurred at about 2.45am.

His injuries are life threatening, ABC reported.
