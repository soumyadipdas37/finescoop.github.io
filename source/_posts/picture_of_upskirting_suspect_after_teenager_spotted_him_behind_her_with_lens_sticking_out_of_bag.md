Police have released this photo of a smartly dressed man in connection with 'upskirting' - where a camera was hidden inside a bag.

Officers have issued CCTV after a teenager noticed a man behind her and saw a lens protruding from his black bag.

The incident happened in Cheltenham, Glos., between 3.15pm and 3.20pm on Tuesday July 14. 

This is the man police want to speak to in connection with an 'upskirting' incident in Cheltenham, Gloucester

It's believed the man was carrying a bag in which a camera was hidden inside as he followed a teenager in Cheltenham

The frightened teenager noticed a man walking behind her and saw a lens protruding from his black bag

The man was smartly dressed in a beige jacket with matching footwear and dark coloured trousers.

Anyone who recognises the person in the image is asked to call 101 urgently and quote incident 331 of 14 July.
