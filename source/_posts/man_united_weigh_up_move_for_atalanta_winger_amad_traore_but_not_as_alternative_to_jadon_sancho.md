Manchester United are weighing up a move for winger Amad Traore from Atalanta, according to reports.

The 18-year-old made his debut for the Serie A side last season and was part of the club's Champions League squad for their quarter final tie against Paris Saint-Germain.

According to Manchester Evening News, United have contacted the Italians amid competition from other clubs but the youngster is not seen as an alternative to Jadon Sancho.
