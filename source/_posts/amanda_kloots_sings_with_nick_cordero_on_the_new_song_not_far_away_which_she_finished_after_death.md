Nearly two months after her husband Nick Cordero succumbed to his battle with COVID-19, Amanda Kloots released her first duet with him.

Kloots, 38, took to Instagram on Thursday to release the song Not Far Away, which is also the couple's third wedding anniversary.

She also took to Instagram on Wednesday to reveal the lyrics to the song, which her late husband was working on at the time of his death and she finished.
