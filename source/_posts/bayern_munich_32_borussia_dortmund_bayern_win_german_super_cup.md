Bayern Munich have won the German Super Cup after beating Borussia Dortmund in a pulsating game at the Allianz Arena.

Hansi Flick's side took a two-goal lead through Corentin Tolisso and Thomas Muller but goals from Julian Brandt and Erling Haaland brought Dortmund level.

But Bayern's ruthless streak continued as Joshua Kimmich popped up to score a fortunate 82nd minute winner to hand Bayern their second trophy of the season.

Bayern Munich have won the German Super Cup after beating Borussia Dortmund

Joshua Kimmich (on the floor) scored a fortunate winner for Bayern at the Allianz Arena

Bayern took the lead after 18 minutes when Corentin Tolisso fired home from very close range

Tolisso celebrates with Alphonso Davies after he finished off a slick Bayern Munich counter

Thomas Muller doubled the Bayern Munich lead as they looked to be in cruise control early on

But Dortmund were not two goals behind for long as Julian Brandt (L) reduced the deficit

Erling Haaland made it 2-2 early on in the second half with an assured left-footed finish

The Norwegian wonderkid celebrates with team-mates Marco Reus and Thomas Delaney
