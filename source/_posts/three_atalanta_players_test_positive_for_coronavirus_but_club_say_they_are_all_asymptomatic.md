Three Atalanta players have tested positive for COVID-19 but all are asymptomatic and have been isolated, the Serie A club said in a statement on Monday.

The tests were carried out ahead of Atalanta's Monday return to training ahead of the new season, due to start on September 19.

The players were not named, and Atalanta said training went ahead as normal for the rest of the squad.

Three Atalanta players have tested positive for coronavirus but they are all asymptomatic

Atalanta finished third in Serie A last season and reached the quarter-finals of the Champions League, where they lost 2-1 to Paris St Germain in Lisbon.

The club is based in the city of Bergamo which was at the epicentre of the coronavirus outbreak in Italy earlier this year.

Gian Piero Gasperini's squad continued to train as normal ahead of Serie A's return next month

Italy have clarified their position for the start of the Serie A season to fall in line with the beginning of the German Bundesliga. 

Fixtures for the new campaign are set to be released on September 2.

The 2019-20 Serie A season reached its conclusion on August 1 before Atalanta, Juventus, Inter and Napoli continued with involvement in European competitions
