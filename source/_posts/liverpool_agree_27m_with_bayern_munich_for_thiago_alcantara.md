Liverpool have agreed a £27million fee with Bayern Munich for Spanish midfielder Thiago Alcantara.

Jurgen Klopp has been chasing the Spanish international all summer and now appears to be edging closer to finally getting his man.

Thiago will be a major coup for Liverpool with the Champions League winner arriving on Merseyside for a relatively modest fee given he is now in the final year of his contract.

Liverpool want reinforcements to bolster their bid to retain the Premier League.
