Lincoln or Liverpool will host Leicester or Arsenal in the Fourth Round of the Carabao Cup.

Leyton Orient or Tottenham will face Chelsea or Barnsley, with the possibility raised of Jose Mourinho taking on his former club. 

Meanwhile, if Manchester United manage to get past Luton they will play Preston or Brighton 

The ties for this round will be played in the week commencing September 28. 

Liverpool could face Arsenal and Tottenham may play Chelsea in Carabao Cup fourth roundL

Liverpool and Arsenal drew 5-5 in an enthralling encounter in the fourth round last season

Liverpool and Arsenal met at this stage of the competition last season, with the game ending in a 5-5 draw and Jurgen Klopp's side prevailing on penalties. 

Other ties see holders Manchester City or Bournemouth travelling to take on Millwall or Burnley. 

Fleetwood or Everton will host West Ham or Hull. West Brom or Brentford will be up against Sheffield Wednesday.

Elsewhere, Bristol City or Aston Villa will play Stoke or Gillingham. Newport County or Watford will play Morecambe or Newcastle.
