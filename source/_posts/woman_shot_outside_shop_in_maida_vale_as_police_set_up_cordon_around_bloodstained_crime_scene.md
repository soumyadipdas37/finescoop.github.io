A woman in her 30s is in hospital after she was shot outside a shop in Maida Vale, London.

Police have set up a crime scene on Harrow Road after she was gunned down before at 11.50pm yesterday.

The woman was treated at the scene before being taken to hospital. Police await an update on her condition. 

Shocking images show the cordon around a blood stained crime scene near the junction at Ashmore Road. 

Police have set up a crime scene on Harrow Road, London, after a woman in her 30s was shot at 11.50pm yesterday

MPS Westminster said: 'A woman aged in her 30s was shot at around 11.50pm in Harrow Rd near junction with Ashmore Rd. 

'She was treated at the scene before being taken to hospital. We await an update on her condition. A crime scene is in place. No arrest. 

'Witnesses pls call 101 ref 8710/11Sep.'

Metropolitan Police have been approached for comment.
