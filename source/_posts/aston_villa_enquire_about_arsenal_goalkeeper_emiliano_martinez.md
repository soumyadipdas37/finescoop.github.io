Aston Villa have asked about Arsenal goalkeeper Emiliano Martinez but are reluctant to meet Arsenal’s asking price of around £20m.

Villa are in the market for another keeper as Tom Heaton continues his recovery from injury.

They admire Martinez but are considering other options also.

Martinez, despite recent performances, also wants guarantees over his first team place at Arsenal.
