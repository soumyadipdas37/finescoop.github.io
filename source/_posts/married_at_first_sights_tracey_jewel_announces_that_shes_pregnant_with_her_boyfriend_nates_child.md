Former Married At First Sight star Tracey Jewel has announced she is expecting a baby. 

On Saturday, the the 37-year-old reality star posted an Instagram photo with her boyfriend Nate, declaring she was pregnant with their second child. 

'We’re having a baby! We are so happy to share our beautiful news with everyone,' she captioned the post. 

'We're having a baby!' Married At First Sight's Tracey Jewel (left) announces that she's pregnant with her boyfriend Nate's (right) child

'I have always believed in second chances and my second baby I’ve been waiting so long for,' she added. 

'My daughter Grace is going to be an amazing big sister and I’m so so grateful to be experiencing pregnancy + motherhood again with my love Nate x.' 

She also posted a picture of her ultrasounds and a baby onesie that read: 'Coming soon.'
