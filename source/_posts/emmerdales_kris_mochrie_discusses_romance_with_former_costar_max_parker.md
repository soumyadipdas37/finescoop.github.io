Former Emmerdale actor Kris Mochrie has spoken about his romance with his ex co-star Max Parker, who played his brother on the soap.

Kris starred as Lee Posner on the ITV series until he left in October of last year while Max continues to play Lee's brother Luke. 

Speaking to The Mirror about their romance, Kris said: 'It's great to meet someone through work and have so many things in common and an understanding of each other's jobs.

'We didn't actually share any scenes together on Emmerdale but kept in touch after I left.

'I don't want to jinx things. We are seeing how we get on. We had a great time on holiday in Dubai. Let's see how it goes.
