The 12th and final contestant to appear on Strictly Come Dancing 2020 is reportedly YouTube star HRVY.

The 21-year-old is a singer and CBBC presenter, with an album set to be released this November, and The Sun claim he is the mystery 12th star to be unveiled. 

HRVY - real name Harvey Leigh Cantwell - will follow in the footsteps of former internet star contestants Joe Sugg and Saffron Barker - the latter of whom is close friends with him.

The pair have also been linked romantically, with HRVY serenading Saffron on her YouTube channel. The pair have also been on tour together.

The pair have claimed, however, that they are just friends.

HRVY has a following on YouTube of 2.76 million, and 4.6 million Instagram followers. His YouTube videos have been viewed over 500 million times.
