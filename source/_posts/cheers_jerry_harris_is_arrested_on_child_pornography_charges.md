Jerry Harris, the 21-year-old star of Netflix's Cheer series, has been arrested on production of child pornography charges.

Harris was charged in federal court in Chicago on Thursday, the Chicago Tribune reports.

His arrest comes just three days after the FBI raided his home in Naperville, Illinois following allegations he solicited sex and sexually explicit photos from twin brothers who were minors.
