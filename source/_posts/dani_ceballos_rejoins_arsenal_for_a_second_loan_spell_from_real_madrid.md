Arsenal have re-signed midfielder Dani Ceballos from Real Madrid on a season-long loan.

The Spaniard produced some encouraging performances under the guidance of Mikel Arteta in the second half of last season and helped Arsenal to win the FA Cup.

Ceballos had been linked with a return to Real Betis but ended up heading back to north London.
