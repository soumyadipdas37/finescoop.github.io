A 33-year-old man is fighting for his life after being shot in the stomach with a crossbow in west London last night.

Metropolitan Police officers were called to Pield Heath Road near the junction with Copperfield Avenue in Uxbridge by London Ambulance crews just after 8pm.

They discovered the victim with a serious injury inflicted by the crossbow arrow.

He was rushed to hospital, where he remains in a 'life-threatening' condition. 

Scotland Yard has not made any arrests and a cordon remains at the scene.
