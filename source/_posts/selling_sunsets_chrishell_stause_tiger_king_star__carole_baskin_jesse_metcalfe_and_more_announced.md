Season 29 of Dancing With The Stars has been revealed.

This year's star-studded line up features Selling Sunset's Chrishell Stause, Tiger King Star Carole Baskin, Jesse Metcalfe, Nelly and more, according to People.

Other celebs who signed on include Backstreet Boys' AJ McLean, former Bachelorette star Kaitlyn Bristowe, Skai Jackson, Anne Heche, The Real's Jeannie Mai, and Nev Shulman, host of Catfish.

Athletes include NBA star Charles Oakley, Olympic figure skater Johnny Weird and former NFL player Vernon Davis. 

Monica, the head coach from the hit Netflix show Cheer is also signed up for this season. 

Season 29 of DWTS kicks off on September 14.
