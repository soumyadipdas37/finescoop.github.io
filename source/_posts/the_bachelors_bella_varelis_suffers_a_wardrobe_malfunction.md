Bella Varelis is widely rumoured to be one of Locky Gilbert's finalists on The Bachelor.

And her outfit on Wednesday night's episode certainly won't do anything to dispel the rumours, with the 25-year-old opting for a racy designer wedding gown.  

Posting to Instagram, the reality star gave suffered a wardrobe malfunction in the very revealing dress ahead of hometown episodes. 

Lady in white: In Wednesday night's episode Bella Varelis is set to wear a sheer designer wedding gown

In the photo, Bella looks glamorous in a white lacy dress that features a sheer skirt and intricate beading. 

Her hair is loose in soft curls and she has a full face of makeup including pink eyeshow and a pink pout. 

The showstopping dress is the 'Le Claudel' gown by bridal and red carpet label J.Andreatta.

The line was launched by Sydney-based designer Jessica Andreatta in 2018.  

According to the Queensland Times one of the designer gowns will set you back anywhere between $8000- $25,000.  

Bella's post comes ahead of the highly anticipated hometown visits, which will see Locky grilled by Bella's protective father.

Posting alongside the picture, Bella revealed that the former Survivor star was very well prepared to meet her father. 

'Locky learned to deal with high-pressure situations on survivor,' she wrote.

'Who knew that would be gearing him up to meet a protective Greek dad. p.s. dad is actually a big softie.' 

The Bachelor continues Wednesday 7:30pm on Channel 10.
