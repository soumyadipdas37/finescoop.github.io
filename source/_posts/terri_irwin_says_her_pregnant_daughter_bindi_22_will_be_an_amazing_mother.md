Terri Irwin is expecting her first grandchild in 2021. 

And the Australia Zoo matriarch, 56, spoke proudly of her pregnant daughter Bindi, 22, in a tweet shared on Sunday.

She posted a photo of herself with her arm around her beaming daughter to mark National Daughter Day.

'She is the most wonderful person': Terri Irwin (left) paid tribute to her pregnant daughter Bindi, 22, on Sunday 

'I know @BindiIrwin will be an amazing mother, because she is the most wonderful daughter in the world,' Terri wrote.

'She always gives me the best advice. Most recently, she said to me, "Be sure to revel in your achievements, and not just worry about your next challenge." 

Last week, Bindi and her husband, Chandler Powell, revealed they were expecting a daughter in a sweet gender reveal.

It's a girl: Last week, Bindi and her husband Chandler Powell revealed they are expecting a daughter who is 'as healthy as can be'. She shared a photo of herself cuddled up to an Aldabra tortoise at her home in Australia Zoo, Queensland to Instagram

The mother-to-be, 22, shared a photo to Instagram of herself cuddled up to an Aldabra tortoise at Australia Zoo. 

Bindi wrote in the caption: 'Baby girl, you are our world.

'Our beautiful daughter is now about the same size as a hatchling Aldabra tortoise and is as healthy as can be.

Baby makes three: Bindi and Chandler revealed their baby news last month on Instagram, with the pair holding up a baby sized Australia Zoo khaki uniform. 'Baby Wildlife Warrior due 2021,' she excitedly announced in the caption

'We can't wait for her arrival next year,' the wildlife warrior added.

Chandler also shared the same sweet photo, and wrote in the caption: 'Our little girl and my beautiful wife are my entire world.

'I'm so excited to meet our daughter when she’s born next year and to see the incredible person she will grow up to be ❤️.'

Just married! The young couple were married on March 25, in a makeshift ceremony at Australia Zoo, just before Covid pandemic lockdown

The couple revealed their baby news last month, sharing a photo to Instagram of themselves holding up a baby-sized Australia Zoo uniform.

'Baby Wildlife Warrior due 2021,' Bindi excitedly announced in the caption.

The young lovebirds married on March 25 at a small ceremony at Australia Zoo, after the COVID-19 pandemic forced them to cancel their original plans.

They met close to seven years ago, in November 2013, when Chandler, who hails from Florida, was touring Australia for a wakeboarding competition.
