Bayern Munich midfielder Michael Cuisance is closing on an £18million move to Leeds United.

The Frenchman has been given permission to speak to Leeds after telling the European champions he wanted more game time.

The 21-year old playmaker cost Bayern £9m when he joined from Monchengladbach last year and has been watched by Newcastle United.

Midfielder Michael Cuisance has been given permission to speak with Leeds over £18m deal

Leeds are discussing a five year contract for the France U20 international.

Leeds also want a winger with Gyrano Kerk from Utrecht and Leicester City’s Demarai Gray on their wishlist.

Meanwhile, Bayern are considering a bid for Norwich City right-back Max Aarons after missing out on Ajax full-back Sergino Dest who is joining Barcelona.
