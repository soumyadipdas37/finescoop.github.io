The Duke and Duchess of Cambridge led public tributes to Prince Harry on his 36th birthday.  

Prince William and Kate Middleton shared a throwback snap of Harry beating them both in a running race at a joint public engagement in 2017, alongside the simple caption: 'Wishing a very happy birthday to Prince Harry today!' 

The Queen, 94, and Prince Charles, 71, shared their own social media tributes, with each choosing photos of Harry laughing at public engagements.   

Prince Harry will celebrate his birthday with wife Meghan Markle and their son Archie in Santa Barbara, where they recently bought their first US home.
