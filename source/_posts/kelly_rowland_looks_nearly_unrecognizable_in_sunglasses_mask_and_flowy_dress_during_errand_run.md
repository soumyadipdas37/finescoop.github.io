Former Destiny Child member Kelly Rowland made an appearance in Beyonce's stunning new video for Brown Skin Girl that debuted just over a week ago.

And on Thursday Rowland was spotted taking care of some errands while out and about in Los Angeles.

She looked nearly unrecognizable wearing a protective mask, amid the still ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, and large, white-rimmed sunglasses that would have made a fashionista from the 1970s proud. 

On the go: Kelly Rowland stepped out to take care of some errands in Los Angeles on Thursday

Between the sunglasses and mask, much of Rowland's face was covered up.

For the outing, she opted to wear a short-sleeve, baggy black dress that draped down to her ankles and nearly covered up her matching black sandals. 

The Georgia native pulled her long raven tresses back into a tight bun and with a part in the middle.

Who's that girl? Rowland, 39,  looked nearly unrecognizable in retro white sunglasses, protective mask and a short-sleeve, baggy black dress that draped down to her ankles

Reality world: Rowland has spent a good deal of her time Down Under serving as a judge and a coach on The Voice Australia since 2017

Rowland, 39, has spent a good deal of her time Down Under serving as a judge and a coach on The Voice Australia since 2017. 

Throughout her career, Rowland has sold over 30 million records as a solo artist, and another 60 million records during her run with Destiny's Child, which helped make them among the best-selling girl groups of all time, with hits like Say My Name and Bootylicious.

The group, that oftentimes featured Beyonce as the lead, released four studio albums beginning in 1997 until their disbandment in 2006. 

Mega-stars: Rowland and Beyonce enjoyed massive success in Destiny's Child; she former group, that at the time included LeToya Luckett and LaTavia Roberson, are pictured in 1999

Rowland and Beyonce have remained friends and have reunited on various projects over the years, including her appearance Brown Skin Girl, which also featured the likes of Beyonce and Jay-Z's eight-year-old daughter Blue Ivy Carter, Naomi Campbell and Lupita Nyong'o.     

Along with her career in music, television and film, Rowland is a proud mother of five-year-old son Titan that she shares with her husband Tim Weatherspoon.

The couple reportedly started dating back in 2011, which led to the announcement of their engagement onThe Queen Latifah Show in December 2013.

They were eventually married in Costa Rica in May 2014.
