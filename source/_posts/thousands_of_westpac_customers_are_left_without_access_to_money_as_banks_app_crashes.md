Thousands of Westpac customers have been left with access to funds with the bank's app and website going down on Monday. 

The issue was acknowledge by the bank at about 10am on Monday morning.  

'There are currently issues with Westpac Live Online and Mobile Banking - we're working to fix these ASAP and will keep you updated here,' the company tweeted.

Customers posted their frustrations online with many tweeted they were without money. 

'Damn, I need to get out of the habit of only transfering as much as I need for the day to my debit card. Now I can't buy even my lunch today,' one person wrote. 

'Yeah, no kidding. Can't access it on the iPhone, the iPad or the website and I can't remember my 3 digit access code for phone banking. All I want is to know what my bloody balance is!' another said. 

'As if life in Victoria isn't bad enough right now. Can't even get into our bank accounts. Does it ever end,' added a third. 

Daily Mail Australia has contacted Westpac for comment.
