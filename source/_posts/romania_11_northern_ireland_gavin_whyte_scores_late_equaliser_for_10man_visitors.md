Gavin Whyte scored a late equaliser with a header as ten-man Northern Ireland drew 1-1 with Romania in the Nations League.  

George Puscas had put Romania in the lead with 25 minutes gone.

Northern Ireland played much of the game with ten men after Josh Magennis was sent off in the 39th minute.

Arsenal youngster Daniel Ballard was given his senior debut for Ian Baraclough's side, while goalkeeper Bailey Peacock-Farrell made some outstanding saves.
