Jamie Murray and Neal Skupski's US Open run ended with a quarter-final loss to Bruno Soares and Mate Pavic in the men's doubles.

Murray won the title with Soares four years ago but it was the Brazilian who had the last laugh here in a 6-2 7-6 (4) victory.

Murray and Skupski made a poor start, losing the first four games, but matched their opponents in the second set and had three set points at 5-4 before going down in the tie-break.

The pair's loss leaves Joe Salisbury, playing with American Rajeev Ram, as the only British player left in the tournament.

The Australian Open champions face eighth seeds Wesley Koolhof and Nikola Mektic in the semi-finals.
