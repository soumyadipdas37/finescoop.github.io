Wolves have confirmed Nuno Espirito Santo has penned a new three-year deal to remain as the club's manager. 

Sportsmail reported recently that Nuno had been waiting from the green light from his agent to officially put pen to paper - and he has now pledged his future to the club for the long term.

The news comes the day before Wolves open the new Premier League campaign at Sheffield United.
