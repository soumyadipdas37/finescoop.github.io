Striker Hal Robson-Kanu scored his first League Cup goal for over 10 years as West Brom beat League Two Harrogate in the Carabao Cup.

Robson-Kanu rifled in a 25-yard screamer just four minutes after Rekeem Harper blasted in a thunderbolt to put the home side ahead.

Callum Robinson wrapped up the win in the 77th minute.
