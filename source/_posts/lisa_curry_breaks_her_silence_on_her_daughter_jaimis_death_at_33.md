Lisa Curry has released a heartbreaking statement about the death of her daughter Jaimi, who lost her battle with a long-term illness on Monday at the age of 33.

The 58-year-old former competitive swimmer spoke of her 'unbearable' pain in a gut-wrenching Instagram post on Tuesday morning.

'Our hearts are completely broken. Our beautiful daughter Jaimi has lost her battle with a long-term illness and passed away peacefully in hospital yesterday morning with her loving family by her side,' she began.
