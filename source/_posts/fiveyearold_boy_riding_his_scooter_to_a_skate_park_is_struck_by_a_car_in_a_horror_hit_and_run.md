A five-year-old boy has been struck by a car in a horror hit and run while riding a scooter to a skate park. 

Emergency services were called to Leanyer, in Darwin's northern suburbs, on Monday at 5.25pm to reports a young child had been hit by a vehicle.  

The driver continued north on Vanderlin Drive, failing to stop and render assistance.

Bystanders rushed to his aid and he was taken to hospital, where he was treated for non-life threatening injuries.

Police believe the car involved was a silver or grey sedan. 

Anyone who can assist police with identifying the vehicle or the driver involved is urged to contact them.
