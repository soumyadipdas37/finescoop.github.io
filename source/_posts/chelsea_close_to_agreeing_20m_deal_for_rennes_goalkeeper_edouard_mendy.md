Chelsea are close to agreeing a £20million deal for Rennes goalkeeper Edouard Mendy.

The 28-year-old was pictured leaving a meeting with the Rennes club chiefs and then did not take part in a training session with the rest of the squad.

Recent reports have suggested that Mendy will join Stamford Bridge on a five-year deal as Frank Lampard's long search for a goalkeeper finally pays off.
