A 17-year-old boy has drowned at a beach near Port Macquarie in New South Wales. 

The boy got into difficulties while swimming with his family at Diamond Head, about 40km south of Port Macquarie, at about 1pm on Wednesday.

Police officers pulled him from the water and started CPR, before paramedics arrived and took over. 

Sadly, he died at the scene.

A report will be prepared for the coroner.
