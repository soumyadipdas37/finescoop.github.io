France host Croatia in Tuesday night's UEFA Nations League action as group A3 leaders Portugal visit Sweden.

France bagan their campaign with a 1-0 victory over Sweden, courtesy of Kylian Mbappe's first-half goal at the Friends Arena in Solna. 

Meanwhile Portugal recorded an emphatic 4-1 win over Croatia at Estádio Do Dragão, with Joao Cancelo opening the scoring with a thunderbolt, followed by goals from Diog Jota and Joao Felix. 

Bruno Petkovic pulled one back in second-half stoppage time but Andre Silva netted a fourth with the lfinal kick of the game as Fernando Santos' side made a perfect start in Cristiano Ronaldo's absence.
