A man has been shot dead while another man has been found with gunshot wounds as a police seige is underway in Sydney's inner west.

A man aged in his 30s was found with gunshot wounds to his chest in Lewisham around 1pm on Friday and died while being rushed to hospital.

A 50-year-old man was also found with bullet wounds around 4pm in Victoria Street.

A major police operation has since been carried out into the area with dogs and a riot squad. 

A major police operation is being carried out in Lewisham in Sydney's west on Friday after a man was shot dead and another was found with bullet wounds

Paramedics are seen at the scene in Lewisham on Friday afternoon 

Heavily armed police have been surrounding a property on Victoria Lane for close to an hour, Nine News reported.

There is reportedly nobody inside the house. 

The shooting victim was found in Henry Street by distressed neighbours who called emergency services. 

Paramedics are currently treating the second gunshot victim.
