She announced she was pregnant with her third child at the beginning of lockdown.

And pregnancy hormones got the better of Rochelle Humes, 31, on Friday, when her eldest daughter Alaia, seven, started at a new school following the extended break. 

The TV presenter and singer, broke down in tears as she told her followers how she was feeling on Instagram. 

Emotional morning: Pregnant Rochelle Humes, 31, broke down in tears on Instagram on Friday after dropping her eldest daughter, Alaia, seven, off at school 

Mummy's brave girl: Alaia Humes, seven, seemed to be excited about her return to school, but Rochelle felt sad she couldn't go into the school to settle her in 

Wearing a grey turtleneck jumper, Rochelle opened up about the heartache of change in the Humes family household.

She said: 'A lot of change for us at the minute as a family - new house, new area but, the most change for my Alaia starting a brand new school after lockdown. 

'I’m so proud of how brave she is and so ready for a new adventure, so odd for me to not being able to go in and make sure she’s okay but I guess that’s the new "normal" for you.

'I held it together and as soon as the door shut I lost it. Being a parent is never easy...please tell me it’s not just me? She’s totally fine by the way and SO excited. I think I got more upset because Marv is away too'

'Being a parent is never easy... Please tell me it's not just me?': Rochelle opened up to her followers about the struggles of parenting

Happy days: As she returned from the school run, Rochelle was instantly cheered up by the sight of her youngest, Valentina, three, dancing around in a Spiderman outfit

As she returned from the school run, Rochelle was instantly cheered up by the sight of her youngest, Valentina, three, dancing around in a Spiderman outfit.

She was captured dancing and singing along to Motivation by Normani. 

The youngster was making music videos with Rochelle's friend Maurice Flynn, proving the apple doesn't fall to far from the tree as she follows in the footsteps of her talented parents. 

The ex Saturdays singer married fellow musician, Marvin Humes, 35, in 2012 at Blenheim Palace in Oxfordshire. 

The couple are expecting their first son in October 2020 and recently held a baby shower celebrating the impending birth.

In an interview with Loose Women, Rochelle joked: 'Every time I go for a scan, I get them to double check his bits because I'm just not convinced I make boys!'
