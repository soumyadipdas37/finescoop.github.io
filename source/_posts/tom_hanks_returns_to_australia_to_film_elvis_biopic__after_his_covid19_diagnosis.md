Tom Hanks announced his coronavirus diagnosis while on Australia's Gold Coast filming Baz Lurhmann's Elvis Presley biopic in March. 

And now, the Castaway star, 64, is back in Queensland to resume filming on the hotly anticipated movie. 

On Tuesday, the Hollywood heavyweight landed at Coolangatta airport in the Sunshine State.

Tom put safety first, covering his famous face with a black mask as he deplaned on the tarmac. 

The actor wore a black and white Adidas tracksuit and a pale cap along with a pair of sneakers.   

He carried a typewriter well as a travel mug, and was surrounded by airport staffers dressed in protective gear. 

Tom was met by Queensland health officials at the airport and had a police escort to the Oracle Resort in Broadbeach.
