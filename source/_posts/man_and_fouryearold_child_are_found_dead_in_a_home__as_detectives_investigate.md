A man and four-year-old boy have been found dead in a home in far-north Queensland sparking a police investigation. 

Officers were called to the property in the suburb of Rossville, north of Cairns, on Friday morning. 

A crime scene has been established with detectives launching an investigation into the circumstances surrounding the deaths of the man, 46, and the boy.
