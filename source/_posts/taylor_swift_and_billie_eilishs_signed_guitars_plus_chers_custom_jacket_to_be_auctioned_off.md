Musicians such as Taylor Swift, Billie Eilish and Cher are donating some of their most iconic items to benefit the MuisCares charity.

Julien's Auctions announced the items that will go on the auction block at their MusiCares Foundation Charity Auction, which takes place Wednesday, September 9.

Advanced online bidding is already under way at JuliensAuctions.com, with the items available to be viewed by the public next week.

Auction: Musicians such as Taylor Swift, Billie Eilish and Cher are donating some of their most iconic items to benefit the MuisCares charity

Taylor Swift's contribution is a signed Gibson American Eagle LG-2 acoustic guitar, which is expected to sell for between $2,000 and $3,000.

Billie Eilish is putting up a signed Fender California Coast black ukulele with yellow abstract pattern, which is projected to fetch between $2,000 and $4,000.

Cher is putting on the block a custom black and silver jacket with crystal and stone designs, with a custom microphone pouch, estimated to sell between $4,000 and $6,000.
