Kylian Mbappe scored the only goal as France scraped past Sweden 1-0 in their opening Nations League match.

The Paris Saint-Germain forward struck in the first half from a tight angle past Sweden's Robin Olsen.

Antoine Griezmann missed a last-minute penalty over the bar after a foul from Victor Lindelof.
