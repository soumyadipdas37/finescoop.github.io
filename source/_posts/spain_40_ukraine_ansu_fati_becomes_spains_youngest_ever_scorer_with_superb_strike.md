Ansu Fati became Spain's youngest ever goalscorer as he helped his side beat Ukraine 3-0. 

Sergio Ramos put Spain ahead from the penalty spot after Fati was brought down in the area. 

Ramos then scored again before Fati, aged 17 years and 311 days, added to Spain's lead with an excellent strike. 

In the second half new Manchester City signing Ferran Torres made it 4-0 with his first international goal.
