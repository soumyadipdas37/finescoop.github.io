Saudi Arabia has convicted eight people of killing journalist Jamal Khashoggi and has given them jail sentences of up to 20 years. 

Five people charged in the  Khashoggi case have been jailed for 20 years, while the other three have been handed sentences of between seven and 10 years, according to a report by Al Arabiya, citing the Saudi Public Prosecution.
