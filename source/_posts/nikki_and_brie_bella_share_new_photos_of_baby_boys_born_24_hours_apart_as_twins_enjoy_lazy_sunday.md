They recently gave birth within 24 hours of each other.

And on Sunday, twins Nikki and Brie Bella posted new photos of their babies to Instagram.

Brie shared a snap of son Buddy in a brown onesie with white stitching made to look a like a football, while Nikki showed off son Mateo wearing a stripy onesie.

Family Sunday: Brie Bella shared a new photo to Instagram on Sunday, showing herself with husband Daniel Bryan, daughter Birdie, three, and newborn son Buddy, born September 1

'Sunday laughs': Brie's twin sister Nikki also updated fans with some new pics of her baby boy Mateo, born August 31

The former pro wrestlers, 36, who turned their sports careers into reality TV fame with The Bellas, are doting on their new arrivals.

Nikki gave birth to her first child on August 31. She shares Mateo with her fiance, the DWTS pro dancer Artem Chigvintsev.

Brie delivered Buddy on September 1, and he's a baby brother for daughter Birdie, three, whom she shares with husband and WWE star Daniel Bryan, 39. 

Cousins: Buddy was dressed in a brown onesie with white stitching made to look a like a football, while Mateo wore a stripy onesie and his hair was brushed up at the front

Siblings: Buddy's older sister Birdie clearly dotes on her new brother

Brie has been married to Daniel since April 2014 and while she is retired, he continues to wrestle in the WWE SmackDown brand on Friday nights.

Brie announced her retirement from the WWE in March 2019 after 11 years and her twin sister Nikki also stepped away from the ring at the same time. 

The sisters now star on the reality TV series Total Bellas and have their own podcast called The Bellas.
