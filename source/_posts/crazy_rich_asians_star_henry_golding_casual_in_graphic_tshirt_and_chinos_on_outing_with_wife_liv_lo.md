California may still have restrictions in place to slow the spread of COVID-19.

But that doesn't stop Henry Golding and his wife Liv Lo from continuing to enjoy dining out under the new rules in place.

The Crazy Rich Asians star, 33, and his TV presenter wife, 35, were spotted once again on Monday grabbing lunch out in Los Angeles.

Golding garnered global fame as one of the breakout stars of Crazy Rich Asians.

In July, it was announced he's lending his voice talent to the Paramount animated feature The Tiger's Apprentice.

He will voice the tiger named Mr. Hu, the protagonist in the 2003 best-selling children's book by Laurence Yep. 

The shape-shifting, talking big cat takes a young boy in San Francisco under his wing, introducing him to a magical world that includes an ancient phoenix, a dragon and a clan of evil-doers.
