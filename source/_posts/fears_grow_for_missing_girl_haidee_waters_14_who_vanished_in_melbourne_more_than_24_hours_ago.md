MISSING: Haidee Waters

A desperate search is underway for a 14-year-old girl who vanished more than 24 hours ago.

Fears are growing for Haidee Waters, who was last seen in Montrose, east of Melbourne, at 10pm on Monday.

She is 165cm tall, has long straight brown hair and is slim. She has braces and was wearing a grey and red hoodie and white trainers on Monday night.

She is known to visit Chirnside Park, Lilydale and Mooroolbark.

Anyone with information should call Mooroolbark Police Station on 03 9725 9999.
