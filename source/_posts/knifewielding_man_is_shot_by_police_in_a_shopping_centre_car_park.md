A knife-wielding man has been shot by police in a shopping centre car park.

The terrifying encounter occurred outside of Lilydale Market in northeast Melbourne on Tuesday morning.

Victoria Police were called to the marketplace after a man armed with a knife was reported on Hutchinson Street around 8.30am. 

Victoria Police shot the man and he received an injury to his upper body.

He is receiving medical treatment at the scene.

Police and emergency services remain on the scene at Lilydale. 

The incident is currently under investigation and is being overseen by Professional Standards Commands.
