Marcelo Bielsa has confirmed that he will stay on as Leeds United manager next season.

The Argentine was out of contract this summer and, while the club were always confident he would commit to a new deal, no news had been forthcoming.

But, speaking on Thursday ahead of Saturday's trip to Liverpool, Bielsa said: 'I will be working the next season at Leeds United. Everything has been sorted and I'll definitely be here next season.'
