Andrea Pirlo started his tenure as Juventus manager with a 3-0 victory over Sampdoria.

Dejan Kulusevski put Juventus ahead in the 13th minute, with Leonardo Bonucci also scoring in the 78th minute.

Ronaldo, who had earlier hit the crossbar, added a third for Juventus late on.
