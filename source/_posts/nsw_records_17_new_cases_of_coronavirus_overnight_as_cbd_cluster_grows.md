New South Wales have recorded an addition 17 new cases overnight as the CBD cluster grows to 49.

The state's Chief Health Officer Dr Kerry Chant announced the new cases during a press conference on Wednesday.

She said it is expected the CBD cluster would grow, as many attend the city every day, but it is important people are vigilant.

Dr Chant said it is important people come forward and check health websites for locations where COVID-19 cases have attended.
