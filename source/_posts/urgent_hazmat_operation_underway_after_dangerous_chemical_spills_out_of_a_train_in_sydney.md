An urgent hazmat operation is underway after a dangerous chemical spilled out of a train in Sydney.

Tonnes of liquid ammonium nitrate leaked from the goods train at the Enfield rail yarn in Greenacre.

New South Wales Fire and Rescue crews are on the scene. 

The area is being evacuated and an exclusion zone and evacuation is being established.
