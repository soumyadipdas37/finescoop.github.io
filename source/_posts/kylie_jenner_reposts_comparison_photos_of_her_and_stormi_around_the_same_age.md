Kylie Jenner loves to twin with her young daughter Stormi. 

And the 23-yer-old showed there is a strong family resemblance between her and her two-year-old daughter. 

The Kylie Cosmetics mogul reposted a side-by-side comparisons of her and Stormi around the same age, to her Instagram Stories on Sunday. 

Strong genes: Kylie Jenner showed there is a strong family resemblance between her and her two-year-old daughter, sharing a side-by-side comparison to Instagram on Sunday

All the comparison photos of Stormi were from Kylie's Friday Instagram post of her daughter, that she captioned 'that smile.'

In the first post, Kylie seemed to be around her daughter's age, in a light colored fancy dress and matching tulle headband. 

Her dark brown hair was down in loose curls as she beamed a tooth-filled smile at the camera.  

Stormi had her raven tendrils tightly slicked back and pulled up into a ponytail that her mom Kylie decked out in brightly colored barrettes. 

Cheesy smile: Another photo of Kylie pulled from the Kardashian-Jenner archives, shows a young reality TV star, with a short blunt bob haircut and thick bangs 

Mom mode: Kylie shares Stormi with 28-year-old rapper Travis Scott, whom she split from in October of 2019 

She wore a bright orange tee that had an upside down Reese's Puffs logo printed on it and a pair of comfy grey shorts.

To finish off her casual ensemble, Kylie slipped Stormi's feet into a pair of white socks and some fun multi-colored sneakers.   

Stormi was a natural in front of her mom's camera as she posed for the shots the spacious living room of their Holmby Hills, California mansion.

Another photo of Kylie pulled from the Kardashian-Jenner archives, shows a young reality TV star, with a short blunt bob haircut and thick bangs. 

In the eyes: She posed gripping a railing in the black and white photo, with jagged banks and a short bob style, as compared to Stormi climbing up on the couch 

The final photo of Kylie seemed to be from a portrait session, and she seems to be just a bit older in age than the others. 

She posed gripping a railing in the black and white photo, with jagged banks and a short bob style, as compared to Stormi climbing up on the couch.   

Kylie shares Stormi with 28-year-old rapper Travis Scott, whom she split from in October of 2019.
