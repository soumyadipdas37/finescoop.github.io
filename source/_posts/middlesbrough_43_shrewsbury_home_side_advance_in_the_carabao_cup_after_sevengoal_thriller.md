Middlesbrough advanced to the second round of the Carabao Cup after a thrilling 4-3 victory over League One side Shrewsbury.

Scott High put the visitors ahead before Marvin Johnson and a brace from Ashley Fletcher put Neil Warnock's side 3-1 up.

Jason Cummings for Shrewsbury to make it 3-2 before Marcus Tavernier added a fourth and Middlesbrough were able to hold on for a victory despite Rekeil Pyke giving the away side something to fight for.
