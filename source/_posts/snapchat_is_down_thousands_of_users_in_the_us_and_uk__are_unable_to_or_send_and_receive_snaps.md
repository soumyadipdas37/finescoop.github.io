If you are unable to send snaps in Snapchat - you are not alone.

The social media platform appears to be down for users in the US and parts of Europe, with many reporting issues with sending pr receiving snaps.

The outage seems to have started around 11:30am ET and quickly spike to more than 47,000 reports on DownDetector.com.

All parts of the US are experiencing problems, while over in Europe the  UK and parts of The Netherlands are shown in red on DownDector's map.

Friday afternoon is a great time for many people, end of the week with the weekend in sight.

However, when Snapchat experiences an outage, the entire world seems to crumble.

Thousands flocked to DownDector, which monitors issues that may arise with online platforms and services, to share the problems they are experiencing using the app. 

The majority of complaints were aimed at sending snaps, but others are also unable to receive them.
