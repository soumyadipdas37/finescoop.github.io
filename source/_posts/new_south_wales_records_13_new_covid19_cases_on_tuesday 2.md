New South Wales has recorded 13 new COVID-19 cases on Tuesday as a Thai restaurant in Newtown becomes a new cluster. 

Of the 13 new cases to 8pm on Monday night, two are returned travellers in hotel quarantine and 11 are locally acquired and linked to a known case.
