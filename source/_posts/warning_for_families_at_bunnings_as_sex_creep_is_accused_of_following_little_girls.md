A man has been accused of indecently photographing a little girl while she was shopping at Bunnings with her family. 

The eight-year-old girl visited the North Mackay store in Queensland on Saturday September 5. 

Police allege the 25-year-old man took pictures of the girl while she browsed the warehouse with her family. 

Detectives from the Mackay Child Protection and Investigation Unit arrested the man at his Ball Bay home.

They searched the property and allegedly found several images of other girls at the store on a mobile phone. 

The alleged victims are yet to be identified.
