An 11-year-old Gold Coast girl has been missing for 10 days and police hold serious concerns about her welfare.

Laine-Ella Hyland hasn't been heard from or seen since she was at a Southport address on August 24, police say.

An Amber Alert has been issued calling on public help to find her, with police seriously concerned about her wellbeing.

She was previously seen leaving her Main Beach home at 8.30pm on August 18.

Laine-Ella is Caucasian, slim and about 150cm tall with shoulder-length blonde hair and blue eyes. She was last seen wearing a grey hoodie and black shorts.
