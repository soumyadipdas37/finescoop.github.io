Kai Havertz is set to play alongside new team-mate Timo Werner for Germany against Spain tonight after German press claimed Bayer Leverkusen had accepted Chelsea's £89million bid. 

The German sensation has been linked with a move to Stamford Bridge all summer and the club-record deal looks on the verge of completion. 

The 21-year-old will line up for his national team against Spain in their Nations League clash against Spain and will offer Chelsea fans a taste of what to expect by starting with Werner.
