Naomi Osaka recovered from losing eight of the opening nine games to beat Victoria Azarenka 1-6, 6-3, 6-3 in the US Open final on Saturday night. 

Japan's Osaka, 22, claimed her third major after winning the 2018 US Open and 2019 Australian Open. 

The 31-year-old Belarusian was playing in her first Grand Slam final since losing to Serena Williams at the 2013 US Open.
