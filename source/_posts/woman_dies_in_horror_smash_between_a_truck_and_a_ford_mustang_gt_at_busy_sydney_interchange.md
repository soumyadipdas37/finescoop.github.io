A woman has died in a horror crash between a sports car and truck in Sydney's south in wet conditions.

Emergency services were called to the intersection of Coward and Bourke streets in Mascot following the collision around 12.30pm on Wednesday.

A critically injured middle-aged woman was trapped inside the car for a short time before being released but died at the scene.

The truck driver suffered minor injuries and was taken to Prince of Wales Hospital for mandatory testing.

Police are investigating the circumstances surrounding the crash and remain at the scene.

Motorists are urged to avoid the area as several roads in the area are closed.

Bus routes 357, 400, 418 and 420 have also been diverted.

A report will be prepared for the Coroner.
