A 12-day-old baby boy has died after he was mauled to death by a dog.

South Yorkshire Police rushed to a property in the village of Woodlands in Doncaster on Sunday afternoon following reports that a child had been attacked.

The baby was rushed to hospital with serious injuries but died a short time later.

A man, 35, and woman, 27, have since been arrested on suspicion of gross negligence manslaughter.
