Aston Villa have made an offer of £15million plus £5m in adds-ons for Bournemouth striker Callum Wilson, MailOnline understands.

We have been told that the Cherries are likely to hold out for more, but Wilson is expected to move on.

Newcastle are also interested in the England frontman but are yet to table a bid.
