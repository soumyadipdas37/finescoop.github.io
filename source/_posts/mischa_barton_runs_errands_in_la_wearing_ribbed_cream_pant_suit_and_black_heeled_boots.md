She started out as a child actress and found fame as Marissa Cooper on Fox TV's hit series The O.C.

And more recently, Mischa Barton returned to the spotlight as part of MTV's reboot The Hills: New Beginnings.

On Tuesday, the 34-year-old was spotted running some errands in LA with actor boyfriend Gian Marco Flamini.

Stylish star: Mischa Barton stepped out in a ribbed cream pant suit and black heeled boots to run some errands in LA on Wednesday 

The actress was dressed in a ribbed cream long-sleeve top and matching pants.

She stepped out in black heeled ankle boots and carried a black designer purse.

Barton tied her hair back from her face, revealing her darker roots, and covered up with a disposable face mask in line with COVID-19 regulations.

Flamini, who didn't have on a mask, wore a white t-shirt tucked into indigo jeans which he paired with a taupe jacket and black boots.

It's not known how long the two have been dating; Barton split from Australian male model James Abercrombie in August 2019 after more than two years together.

Born in London and raised in New York, Barton divides her time between the U.S. and Europe. 

Her most recent big screen role is as a passenger for a ride share driver played by Stranger Things star Joe Keery in the dark comedy Spree.

The movie, originally shown at Sundance, was released on video on demand last month also features Franke Grande, Lala Kent and David Arquette.
