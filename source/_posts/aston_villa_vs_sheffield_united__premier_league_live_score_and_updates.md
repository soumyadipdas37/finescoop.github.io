Aston Villa host Sheffield United in the first of two games on Monday evening in the Premier League. 

Dean Smith's side did not play last week in the league, but did beat Burton Albion 3-1 in midweek in the second round of the Carabao Cup. 

The Blades, meanwhile, lost 2-0 to Wolves last Monday in their top-flight opener, while they also lost on penalties to Burnley in the Cup in midweek. 

Sportsmail's Tom Caldon will provide all the team news, build-up and live commentary from Villa Park, with kick-off at 6:00pm!
