She's just sold her directorial feature debut Bruised to Netflix in a rumored eight figure deal.

And Halle Berry has also gone pubic with a new romance, confirming she's in a relationship with singer Van Hunt.

On Sunday, the actress, 54, shared a selfie to Instagram in which she's seen with her 50-year-old beau.

'You’ve got to COORDINATE': Berry, 54, shared a selfie to Instagram on Sunday in which she's seen with her new beau Van Hunt, 50, wearing matching black cloth face masks 

The photo and caption served as an encouragement to her social media followers to abide by the wearing mask rule during the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic.

Both Berry and Hunt are seen in the selfie wearing matching black cloth face masks and in the caption, the Monster's Ball Oscar winner wrote: 'You’ve got to COORDINATE. #MasksSaveLives.'

The post comes a week after Berry confirmed her new romance by posting to Instagram a snap in which she's shown planting a kiss on the musician's cheek.

Couple alert: The post comes a week after Berry confirmed her new romance by posting to Instagram a snap in which she's shown planting a kiss on the musician's cheek

Hunt, who hails from Dayton, Ohio, has toured with Kanye West, Mary J. Blige, Coldplay, Dave Matthews Band, Afghan Wigs and The Roots.

He won a Grammy back in 2007 for his work on a cover of the Sly & the Family Stone song Family Affair.

Berry's film Bruised premiered at the Toronto International Film Festival earlier this month.

The drama, which she also stars in, is set in the world of mixed martial arts.

The former Die Another Day Bond girl plays a former MMA fighter who has lost custody of her son and embarks on a mission to regain both her child and her career. 

In the money: Earlier this month, it was reported that Berry's directorial debut, gritty drama Bruised, in which she also stars, had been sold to Netflix for a rumored eight-figure sum 

She was last seen on the big screen in last year's John Wick: Chapter 3 - Parabellum opposite Keanu Reeves. 

Berry has been married three times and divorced three times.

She has two children, daughter Nahla, 12, with ex-boyfriend Gabriel Aubry, and son Mateo, six, with third husband Olivier Martinez.
