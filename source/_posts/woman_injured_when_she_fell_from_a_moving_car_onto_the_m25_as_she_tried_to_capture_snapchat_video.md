A woman luckily avoided serious injury after she fell from a moving car on the M25 as she leaned from a window to grab a video for Snapchat.

The un-named woman fell from the vehicle between junction six and the Clacket Lane Services at 1.30am today into a 'live lane.'

Surrey Police officers Tweeted about the accident. The woman was not badly hurt but the police added that it was lucky 'she wasn't seriously injured or killed.'

The Surrey force's Roads Policing Unit Tweeted: 'The front seat passenger was hanging out the car whilst filming a Snapchat video along the M25. She then fell out the car and into a live lane.

'It is only by luck she wasn't seriously injured or killed. #nowords'

A police spokesman said: 'Officers were called to the M25 between junction six and Clacket Lane Services shortly after 01.30 this morning following reports of a female falling out of a moving vehicle.

'The woman was treated by paramedics at the scene and her injuries were not life-threatening or life-changing.'
