Holland host Italy in their second matchday clash in the UEFA Nations League tonight.

The Dutch side beat Poland in their opening game with Tottenham's Steven Bergwijn scoring the only goal whilst their rivals were able to grab a draw after Stefano Sensi cancelled out Bosnia's Edin Dzeko goal for a 1-1 draw.

Elsewhere, Scotland travel to Czech Republic in a search for their first victory of the competition, following the frustrating 1-1 draw with Israel in their opener.

Northern Ireland welcome Norway in League B clash after gaining a point in their first game following  a draw with Romania as Erling Haaland and Co look to register their first points of the tournament.
