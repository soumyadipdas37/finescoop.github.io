An aerial search is under way for five people missing east of Alice Springs.

The group failed to meet up with friends at a campground at Ruby Gap, 150 kilometres east of the town, over the weekend.

Gabrielle Freedman, 29, Shaygun Narula, 27, Haydn Dodds, 29, Renae Walker, 31 and Jessica Kolic, 29, left Alice Springs in a white Toyota Hilux with NT registration 653414 about 5pm on Saturday.

An aerial search is underway for five missing doctors after the group failed to meet up with friends at a campground at Ruby Gap (pictured), 150 kilometres east of Alice Springs, over the weekend

An aerial search of the Ross Highway, Stuart Highway and Binns Track began at first light on Monday.

Police hold concerns for the welfare of the group as it's unknown what provisions they have with them.

The site is only accessible via a notoriously difficult four-wheel drive track.
